Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your mystery date night.

What does the concierge package fee cover? +
The package fee covers our full concierge planning service, priority table reservations, and secret digital portal access. Food and beverage costs are paid directly to the local venues during your date night.
What if we have dietary restrictions or food allergies? +
After completing your booking, you'll be directed to our Guest Preferences Form where you can specify allergies, dietary needs (e.g., vegetarian, gluten-free, shellfish), and preferred dining atmospheres.
How does the Live Date Portal work? +
You will receive a confidential passcode via email. On date night, simply open the portal on your phone. A live countdown clock will unlock each destination's name, address, and special vouchers right as your evening progresses!
Where are the stops located? +
All itineraries are concentrated in walkable areas of Downtown Annapolis, including historic Main Street, Ego Alley waterfront, and West Street.
