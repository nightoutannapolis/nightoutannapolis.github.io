What is included in the curation fee? +
Your fee covers full itinerary planning, reservations, activity entry passes, and exclusive discounts or vouchers at selected partner locations.
How are dietary restrictions or special accommodations handled? +
After booking, you will complete a quick preference form where you can list allergies, dietary restrictions, or mobility requirements. We tailor every stop to meet your needs.
How do we access our itinerary on the night of the date? +
We send a unique passcode to your email. On your date night, visit the NightOut Portal on your smartphone and enter your code to view your schedule and access vouchers.
Where are the date night locations situated? +
All curated stops are located within close proximity in Historic Annapolis and Eastport, allowing for an easy walk or short drive between destinations.
