How It Works (In 4 Easy Steps!)

You do not have to plan anything. We do all the work for you!

Step 1: Pick Your Day

Go to our website. Pick the day and time you want to go out on your date.

Step 2: Tell Us What You Like

Tell us what food you like and what food you cannot eat. We pick the best spots for you!

Step 3: Get Your Secret Code

We email you a secret passcode. Keep it safe until your date night begins!

Step 4: Go Out and Unlock the Surprise! 🎁

On the night of your date, type your code into the NightOut Portal on your phone.

A timer will count down! When it hits zero, your phone reveals where to go next, your pre-paid tickets, and special coupon codes for free food and drinks!

Questions People Ask Us

Click a question to see the simple answer!

What does our fee pay for? +
It pays for our team to plan your whole night! It also pays for your tickets to fun activities and gets you free drink or dessert vouchers at restaurants.
What if I cannot eat certain foods? +
No problem! After you sign up, you tell us what foods you like or cannot eat. We make sure your dinner spot fits your needs.
How do I see my date night plan? +
We email you a secret code. On your date night, open the Portal page on your phone and type in your code. It shows you where to go!
Where do we go on the date? +
All stops are close to each other in Downtown Annapolis. You can easily walk or drive a short distance between stops!
