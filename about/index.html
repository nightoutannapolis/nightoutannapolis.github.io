Our Purpose & Mission

Bringing romance and spontaneity back to Annapolis while championing local businesses.

NightOut Annapolis was founded with a clear purpose: couples deserve extraordinary, stress-free date nights that turn into lifelong memories, and the best way to deliver that is by showcasing the incredible local culture right here in our community.

✨ Unforgettable Memories

We eliminate planning fatigue and decision stress. The element of mystery brings back genuine excitement, spontaneity, and romantic connection for every date night.

⛵ Supporting Local Merchants

Annapolis is home to iconic historic spots, independent restaurants, and waterfront gems. Every NightOut itinerary directs 100% of date spending straight into local Annapolis businesses.
